Our Story

Youth Voice Foundation was established on 31st August 2021 under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013, as a company limited by shares.

The foundation emerged to drive initiatives that uplift education, social welfare, culture, economy, and medical support for marginalized communities. We proudly hold 12A & 80G certifications and are CSR Form-1 compliant, demonstrating our commitment to transparency and credibility.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive society where all individuals—especially youth—are empowered to thrive, contribute, and lead transformational change for a better nation.
